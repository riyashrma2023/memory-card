How To Play

Quick Instructions

  • Choose a difficulty level and start flipping cards.
  • Only two cards can be revealed at the same time.
  • Matching pairs stay open, mismatches flip back after a moment.
  • Finish the board in the fewest moves and fastest time you can.

Tips

Play Smart

  • The timer begins on your first move, so take a breath first.
  • Try smaller boards to learn symbol positions before going harder.
  • Your best score is saved separately for each difficulty level.
